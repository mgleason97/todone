## Tasks

A task has the following fields:
* Title - concise title of the task, no more than 10 words.
* Description - more detailed explanation of the task. Should still be concise and no more than 100 words.
* Estimated Effort - estimation of the time to complete the task, in minutes. Assume the person assigned to the task is knowledgeable and competent. No learning outside of that which is explicity mentioned in the task would be necessary. 
* Priority - scale from 0 (high) to 2 (low) of how important a task is. 0 is urgent, 1 is something that should be done but is less pressing, 2 is something that is nice to do, but its completion is not critical.
